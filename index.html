Our goal

The aim of Lilibrio is to help people find their perfect book depending on their own taste. We want to do that with the help of the literature lovers who want to help people reconnect with reading by giving them advices on what book they could like and by donating their own books so that people can find books easily and without spending money. When you are a member, you can go on the app and fill a form about what you want to read, and the app will suggest you a book according to your taste, thanks to the advices uploaded by other members.

The founder

HEC Paris graduate, Elisa Raboteau decided to build this project because she always loved to read and believed that helping people finding the right book for them is the first step to help someone love reading.
